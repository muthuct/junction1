Signage and Junction 1

At Junction 1, we offer a full range of sign systems designed to cater for your exact requirements. From fascias - perhaps incorporating illuminated, projected lettering, using acrylic, aluminum or molded letters and full-colour images - spinning signs, plaques and panel and post systems. We provide our customers with a range of signage solutions to meet every need.

We can help you build a consistent brand image across all of your signage; whether you have interior or exterior signs, window graphics, exhibition stands or promotional displays. We also create signage for specific campaigns; for example a taxi wrap or promotional vehicles for your companies new product launch or a suspended banner advertising a sales offer.
